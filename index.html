About RADA ROUGE

Transforming architecture through light, shadow and intention.

RADA ROUGE Lighting Design Consultancy is dedicated to translating architectural vision into meaningful lighting experiences. The studio works closely with architects, interior designers and developers to create solutions that are visually compelling, technically refined and purpose-driven.

We fearlessly compose light and shadow.

Every beam of light, every shadow and every subtle contrast is intentionally crafted to reveal spatial depth, enhance architectural form and create emotional resonance.

At RADA ROUGE, light is not simply illumination. It is a design language that brings architecture to life.

Design Philosophy

The studio believes exceptional lighting design is found in the balance between visibility and mystery, precision and artistry, technology and human experience.

Founder & Design Director

Ada Wong

Ada Wong is an architectural lighting designer with more than eleven years of experience across luxury hospitality, high-end residential, commercial and mixed-use developments.

Her background spans interior design, architectural engineering management and senior lighting design roles in internationally recognised studios. Her work is guided by a sensitivity to Asian aesthetics, global design language and the emotional power of light.
